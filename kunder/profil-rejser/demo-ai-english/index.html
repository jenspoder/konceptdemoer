Profil Rejser — AI Search

Interactive prototypes demonstrating an AI-powered search experience on the homepage.

1. Design-native integration

The search UI is built into the site's visual language — not a generic pop-over widget dropped on top of the page.

2. Results-first, then depth

Most AI search implementations show a loading AI response at the top while results trickle in below. Here it's flipped: you get results immediately, with the option to ask a follow-up or get a deeper explanation if you want it.

3. Actions inside results

Search results aren't just links — they include contextual actions designed to move users into specific journeys. AI search as a conversion surface, not just a navigation tool.
